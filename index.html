Web Design Made Practical

A web developer's design bible, if you will. In ~200 pages, this e-book teaches you web design in a practical way. It touches on concepts you may not have thought of, like creating depth, designing systematically, and so much more. Highly recommend for anyone looking to up their web-design game!

Course

FreeCodeCamp: Javascript Algorithms & Data Structures

Course

Meet Javascript

My first real run-in with Javascript was here in FreeCodeCamp. I enjoyed the learning style in this course, wherein a new concept is introduced, and you immediately have a chance to practice at it before moving on. The final projects are a great exercise in seeing how much you've retained. Be patient with this one!

Book

You Don't Know JS Yet: Get Started by Kyle Simpson

Book

JS From a New Perspective

A comprehensive look at Javascript through the eyes of someone who understands the pain points. The author dives in to many concepts I hadn't previously given enough thought, such as use strict, coercive comparison, and more. This book isn't one you skim through - I recommend you take your time and truly digest the information.
